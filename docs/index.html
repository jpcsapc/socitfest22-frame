    In collaboration with the SoCIT Organizations of Asia Pacific College, we are inviting everyone to join this event this coming August 27, 2022, from 1:00 PM – 4:30 PM. We would love you to come and meet with our fellow members, which will surely make you feel at home and a part of our organizations.

    We’ll tackle many topics related to Information Technology (IT), the industry, and the benefits that you’ll gain if you choose to be a member of one of the SoCIT organizations. You’ve got a lot to choose from: Junior Philippine Computer Society (JPCS-APC), Junior Information Systems Security Association (JISSA), Microsoft Community (APC-MSC), and Gaming Genesis.

    The assembly will be held inside the school, at the APC Multipurpose Hall 1 (MPH1), and on Microsoft Teams, for those who’d prefer to join us online.
